Privacy Policy

The Shakarzahi LLC is committed to safeguarding the private information provided to us by website visitors, suppliers, and other third parties. Shakarzahi LLC places a high focus on protecting your privacy, and we go above and beyond to safeguard the data we have about you. This Privacy Notice outlines the legal basis on which we will process any Personal Information you give us or that we collect about you, how we use it, and how we safeguard it.

Please read the following to learn more about the personal information we collect, how we use it, and how it might be shared with you. Do not use the Website or any of our services if you do not consent to process your Personal Information by us in the manner outlined in this Privacy Notice.

Personal Information

We collect Personal Information from you directly or automatically generated from your use of our Website or services. Even before we have our first direct interaction, we may get Personal Information about you from other sources and third parties.

Personal Data You Provide to Us

If you directly provide us with personal information, we can collect it. For instance, we may obtain Personal Information from you when you interact with the Website, sign up for a newsletter, grant access to certain content in exchange for your Personal Information, submit an employment application to us, complete a "Contact Us" form on the Website or communicate with us by phone, email, or another method. Additionally, we might collect personal information. Additionally, we may collect Personal Information from you at live events and other meetings. When you use our services, such as when you make a purchase, we may also get Personal Information from you.

Your first and last name, email address, phone number, postal address, employer, job title, and any other information that may help us process your request and respond to your inquiries are examples of this type of Personal Information. We use the data we gather to:

  • Identify you.
  • Create, maintain, and service your account.
  • Maintain communication with you.
  • Make payments more accessible for you.
  • Operate, support, and develop our company and the goods and services we offer consumers.

We collect information regarding how you use any of our websites or other digital properties whenever you visit one of them. This includes details about the web pages you visit and the device you are using. Your browser or cookies automatically provide us with this information. We gather this data to enhance your browsing experience and troubleshoot any unforeseen technical issues.

You might occasionally be invited to complete an online survey or questionnaire when visiting Shakarzahi LLC websites or other digital properties. These surveys and questionnaires all requested your ideas, attitudes, interests, and more general information about your internet activity.

How We Use the Personal Information We Collect

Shakarzahi LLC exchanges personal information with third parties as necessary for business operations. We only use this approach in the following specific situations:

Our subsidiaries, related businesses, and other third parties that help us offer you current and prospective future services or for other lawful purposes are given access to your personal information (subject to appropriate confidentiality agreements and our instructions consistent with this privacy notice).

Personal data is given to (a) conform to legal requirements, be in compliance with court orders, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests; (b) enforce applicable Terms of Service, including looking into potential violations of such Terms; and (c) identify, contact, prevent, or otherwise deal with fraud, security, or technical issues or (d) if required or permitted by law, defend against impending threats to the safety, property, or rights of Shakarzahi LLC, its clients, or the general public.

In the event of a corporate reorganization, sale of stock or assets, or other change in corporate control involving Shakarzahi LLC or its affiliated companies, we may disclose and transfer personal information to a third party. This includes any transfers made as part of bankruptcy proceedings or insolvency.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We disclose personal data in the manner listed below:

  • Service Providers: To make our services available to you, we may disclose Personal Information to third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers that carry out tasks or provide services on our behalf and by our instructions.
  • Distributors: To better enable our services, we exchange Personal Information with our distributors;
  • Business Transaction or Restructuring: If we participate in or are otherwise connected with a corporate business transaction, such as if we sell or buy any business or assets, we may disclose your Personal Information to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets; if a third party acquires Shakarzahi LLC or substantially all of its assets, we may disclose your Personal Information to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets; or if we restructure our business, we may disclose your Personal Information to the prospective buyer of such business.

Furthermore, including wireless networks and the Internet, we are not liable for the security of any information you provide to the Website over these systems.

Cookies

When you visit our Website, read our emails, or interact with us in any other way online, we automatically gather certain usage and device information, as is the case with many websites. We frequently use tracking technologies (sometimes referred to as "tracking technologies") to gather this information, including cookies, pixels, Local Stored Objects (such as Flash cookies), web beacons, log file information, and similar tools. We may also employ third-party services to collect this data. The pages you view, the links you click, whether you read emails or click the links inside of them, whether you access the Website from various devices, and other behaviors are all examples of the information we get about how you use our Website. To analyze website traffic and usage trends and learn more about our users' demographics, we may gather analytics data or use third-party analytics tools like Google Analytics.
